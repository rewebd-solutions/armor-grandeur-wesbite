Brief about how your company innovation?

What is the uniqueness of your solution?
Battery management system and control circuits design for high power applications in dynamic operating conditions.
How does your startup generate revenue?
BMS design wanted in various applications like Electric vehicle, Renewable energy systems, Fast charging technology, mobile battery applications etc.
